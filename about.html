A JOURNEY THAT BEGAN WITH ONE CORE BELIEF!

For 37 years, Geetanjali Salon has stood for craftsmanship, care and an unwavering belief that beauty should help you feel more like yourself. What began as a single studio in Green Park, New Delhi, in 1989 is today a 200+ salon chain across 50+ cities, powered by 5000+ hair professionals.

"Beauty should never change who you are. It should help you feel more like yourself."

Our Belief

Every chair at Geetanjali is a place to be heard before you are styled. Our artists are trained to listen — to your hair, your skin, your story — and to translate that into a service that feels personal, never templated.

Three decades on, the belief remains the same: craft that honours the individual, delivered with the consistency only India's most awarded salon chain can promise.

Meet the Founder

Sumit Israni

Founder · Creative Director · Managing Director · Celebrity Hair Designer

Sumit Israni founded Geetanjali Salon in 1989 with one chair, one mirror and a clear point of view: that Indian beauty deserved global craft. Three decades on, he remains the creative compass of the brand.

His scissors have shaped the looks of Michelle Obama, Deepika Padukone and a long roster of public figures — but his proudest legacy is the 5000-strong artist community trained inside Geetanjali.
